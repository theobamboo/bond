📚 glossary & asset impact

₿ Crypto (the canary)

Highly sensitive to DXY. 24/7 liquidity — first to dump when institutions face bond paper losses. If yields spike, BTC 'wicks' down as liquidity vanishes.

📈 Indices & stocks

Yields = discount rate. Higher yields = future profits worth less today. Tech & growth hit hardest (long-duration cash flows).

⚡ Futures & options

Futures: high VIX widens spreads → traders stopped out. Options: VIX pumps IV, premiums expand, vol trades get interesting.

🏅 Commodities

Typically inverse to DXY. Strong dollar = weaker gold/oil. In true systemic stress, gold can decouple as final store of value.

🧾 Training summary: day 1

  • 01.The core inverse: Bond prices and yields move opposite. Spiking yields = bond discounts; falling yields = bond premiums.
  • 02.The VIX accelerant: High VIX makes market makers widen spreads → 'clogged pipes' become lethal for leveraged traders.
  • 03.The DXY vacuum: Rising dollar pulls liquidity, forcing leveraged loops to unwind — crypto & risk assets crash.
